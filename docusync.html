About Docusync

Docusync is a document management system designed for secure document storage and sharing developed by our team using Next.js, Strapi, TailwindCSS, and PostgreSQL, Docusync offers a seamless and user-friendly experience for managing documents efficiently. Users can upload, organize, and share files with ease, ensuring streamlined collaboration.

Technologies used in Docusync :

Frontend : Nextjs, Tailwindcss

Backend : Nodejs, Strapi, PostgreSQL
