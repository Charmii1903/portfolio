About WiseCart

WiseCart is your one-stop solution for finding the perfect gifts for any occasion. We provide a wide range of products, carefully curated to meet your gifting needs. Whether it's a birthday, anniversary, or festival, WiseCart has you covered with unique and thoughtful gift options.

In whirlwind 36 hours we developed this in HachNUthon 5.0 a Wisecart with a chatbot "Wisebot" AI for assitance which is the main feature of this webapp for better shopping experience by our team .

Technologies used in WiseCart :

Frontend : Reactjs , CSS

Backend : Python , MongoDB
